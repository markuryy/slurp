# Slurp

Slurps video and audio from a UVC capture card and spits it out on your screen.

## Requirements

- macOS 14.0+
- A UVC capture card

## Usage

Plug in your capture card and launch Slurp. Select your video and audio devices.

Move your mouse into the window to reveal controls:

- **Volume slider** to adjust audio
- **Camera button** or **Cmd+S** to save a screenshot to your Desktop
- **Device picker** to switch video/audio sources (only shows when multiple devices are detected)

Double click to go fullscreen. Escape to exit fullscreen.

## Building

Open `Slurp.xcodeproj` in Xcode and hit Run.
